Credentials

Dr. Ipsen is certified by the American Board of Psychiatry and Neurology. She is a Fellow of the American Psychiatric Association.

Dr. Ipsen received her medical degree from the University of Rochester Medical School in Rochester, NY. She completed her residency training in psychiatry at the University of Colorado in Denver, Colorado. She has been in practice in the Albany area for over 20 years.

In addition to her full-time private practice, Dr. Ipsen is on the volunteer faculty at Albany Medical Center; she supervises psychiatry residents in various psychotherapy modalities including psychodynamic, cognitive-behavioral, and interpersonal.

Dr. Ipsen is a member of the Capital District Branch APA and has served on the Ethics Committee.
