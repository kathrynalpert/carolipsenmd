Care Philosophy

I provide high quality, personalized, confidential psychiatric care. I use an integrated approach and pay special attention to relationship issues and current life stresses which may be contributing to the problem.

Anxiety and depression problems are generated by biological, interpersonal and developmental factors, which interact in a complex manner. I feel that medication alone often is not adequate treatment and that gaining some understanding of what is causing the mood disorder is crucial in obtaining long-term relief. My focus is on helping people with achieving a greater understanding of their difficulties and on working to develop healthier ways of coping with stress and finding greater satisfaction in their personal and professional lives.
